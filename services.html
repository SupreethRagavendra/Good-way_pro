Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What documents are required for passport application?

For new passport application, you need proof of identity (Aadhaar/Voter ID), proof of address (utility bill/rent agreement), birth certificate, and passport-size photographs. Our team will guide you through the complete list based on your specific case.

How long does PAN card processing take?

New PAN card applications typically take 5-10 working days. Corrections and duplicate PAN cards may take 7-15 days depending on the verification process. We provide real-time status updates throughout the process.

Do you charge extra for train ticket bookings?

We charge minimal service fees for train bookings. Our charges are transparent and clearly mentioned before booking confirmation. As an IRCTC authorized agent, we ensure you get tickets at official rates.

Can you help with urgent document processing?

Yes, we offer expedited processing for urgent cases wherever government provisions allow. Additional charges may apply for tatkal or urgent processing services. Contact us for specific timeline requirements.

Do you provide doorstep document collection?

Yes, we offer doorstep document collection and delivery services within our service areas. This service is available for most government document services with minimal additional charges.
